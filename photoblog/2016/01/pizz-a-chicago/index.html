Pizz'A Chicago

On: January 11, 2016, by: Juan Orozco

Photo a day

Food photography of my favorite pizza...

The BEST gluten free pie I've had so far. Pizz'A Chicago in Santa Clara.

Pepperoni, cheese, sausage, garlic, mushrooms, and tomatoes. Sauce is very flavorful. Gluten-free crust is on the thin crispy side but I like that.

Also, food photography is hard!
