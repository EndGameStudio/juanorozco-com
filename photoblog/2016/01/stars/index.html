On: January 19, 2016, by: Juan Orozco

Photo a day

One thing I learned is that "infinity" is relative to the Sony kit lens. As I was setting up, I set the focus to manual and focused all the way to infinity. I kept getting shots that were very blurry. After some experimenting in the subfreezing temperatures of my backyard, I realized that the infinity is a "range". WEIRD. I suspect it is due to this lens being a zoom lens; I bet a prime wouldn't do this.
