Photo a day

When I finally bought my Sony a6000, the fiery passion for photography was reignited within me. I started the photo a day with the intention of just using my a6000. I don't have a wide angle lens that I can use but the GoPro takes really good photos. So, I decided that I would use any camera at my disposal to take photos for Photo A Day. This includes...

  • my a6000, the kit lenses it came with and any lens I can use with an adapter (reusing my FD mount lenses is so much fun)
  • GoPro Hero 4
  • iPhone 6
